# Wejha Brand Identity (الهوية البصرية لمشروع وجهة 2026)

This document details the visual identity of **Wejha (مشروع وجهة)** as extracted from the official brand identity documentation.

---

## 1. Brand Overview & Philosophy (رؤية وفلسفة الهوية البصرية)

The visual identity of the **Wejha** project has been reconstructed to give it a fresh start that reflects its true impact and future ambitions. The design process focused on building a **flexible, clear, and scalable design system** that maintains the simplicity and strength of the message, delivering a modern visual experience that builds trust and enhances communication with youth.

> **Visual Reconstruction Goals:**
> *   **Flexibility & Scalability:** A design system that grows with the project.
> *   **Clarity:** Keeping the core message clear and prominent.
> *   **Modernity:** A contemporary aesthetic that resonates with youth.
> *   **Impact:** A comprehensive enhancement that boosts presence, visibility, and credibility.

## 5. Typography (الخطوط والطباعة)

The brand uses **DIN Next LT** (specifically **DIN Next LT Arabic**) as its primary typeface. This font is selected for its clean, geometric, and modern look that is highly legible in both Arabic and English scripts.

### Font Weights
The design system employs three primary weights of **DIN Next LT**:
1.  **Heavy / Bold (الثقيل):** Used for main titles and high-emphasis headers.
2.  **Regular (العادي):** Used for subtitles, body text headers, and medium-emphasis elements.
3.  **Light (الخفيف):** Used for descriptive paragraphs, labels, and secondary details.

### Text Hierarchy (الهيكل التنظيمي للنصوص)
To maintain visual consistency across all digital and print platforms, the following size hierarchy is specified:

*   **Main Title (عنوان رئيسي):** `60 pt` (Heavy / Bold)
*   **Subtitle / Secondary Header (عنوان ثاني):** `50 pt` (Regular / Bold)
*   **Section Title / Sub-subtitle (عنوان فرعي):** `40 pt` (Regular)
*   **Body Paragraph 1 (مقطوعة نص - كبـير):** `30 pt` (Regular / Light)
*   **Body Paragraph 2 (مقطوعة نص - صغيـر):** `20 pt` (Light)

## 6. Visual Brand Assets & Logo Usage (الشعار والتطبيقات البصرية)

### Logo Variations
The logo features a custom geometric Arabic logotype **"وجهة"** with the sub-text **"مشروع 2026"**. It is designed to work in high-contrast environments:
*   **Dark Blue Logo** on Off-White background (Standard).
*   **White Logo** on Deep Navy Blue background.
*   **White Logo** on Bright Orange background.
*   **Orange Logo** on White background.

### Application Mockups
The visual identity is implemented across several platforms:
*   **Digital Platform:** Web interface shown as `Wejha Platform.Edu.ly`.
*   **Social Media Templates:** Square templates containing high-contrast text overlaying images (e.g., AI Session, How to Choose a Major, "80% of students have a problem").
*   **Physical Merchandising:** Blue and orange mugs, tote bags, pins, notebooks, identity badges, and exhibition booths.
*   **Outdoor Advertising:** Large billboard layouts featuring the primary logo and slogan.
